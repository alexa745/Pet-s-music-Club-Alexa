We welcome all of you

We see a future in which the healing power of music and the company of animals come together to promote happiness, healing, and hope.

In addition to being entertaining, our performances help spread the word about the critical role therapy animals play in enhancing emotional health, lowering stress levels, and promoting healing in people dealing with a variety of difficulties.

Latest Events

New Therapy sessions!

We frequently collaborate with shelters, community centres, and animal therapy organisations to present live performances that unite families, people looking for comfort, and animal lovers.

Original music and covers are incorporated into each of our events to provide a calm environment
where pets and their owners can connect, unwind, and savour the moment.
